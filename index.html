What is Alchemancy?

Alchemancy is a 2-player summoner-style card/board game where players each bring a unique deck of 30 cards, along with one alchemancer. Your goal is to reach your opponent’s laboratory and reduce its health from 20 to 0, while protecting your own laboratory using creatures, effects, structures, and special terrain cards. You’ll have to use your alchemical knowledge to combine raw resources and time your decisions perfectly with the changing lunar phases.

Join the community on Discord to find games and ask me (Moonfassa) for an intro on how to play!

The Board

Build manaducts to collect extra resources from the mana wells and extend your summoning range. The TTS mod is fully automated and even has a deck importer/exporter.

Lunar Clock

Time your plays carefully with the lunar clock. The tides will roll in on the full and new moons, washing away your manaducts and drowning unsuspecting creatures. Several cards are affected by the lunar cycles as well, such as the short lifespans of insect creatures.

Deckbuilder

Conveniently lay out all the cards in the mod and build your deck. Experiment with over 400 cards!
